  Finding: at 500KB the per-detector regex sweep (each detector scans the
  whole buffer) exceeds the latency target. Optimisation path:
   - a true multi-pattern matcher that returns per-pattern matches without
     stealing (Hyperscan/Vectorscan, or RE2::Set for membership pre-filter)

   - head/tail extraction + size gate for very large files (spec ExtractConfig)
